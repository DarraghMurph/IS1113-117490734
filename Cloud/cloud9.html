Platform as a Service (PaaS) - Cloud9

Platform as a Service (PaaS) is a category of cloud computing services that provides a platform allowing customers to develop, run, and manage applications without the complexity of building and maintaining the infrastructure typically associated with developing and launching an app.

Advantages:

  • Allows for higher level programming with reduced complexity
  • Overall development is generally more effective as maintenance and enhancement of the app is easier
  • Facilitates collaboration

Disadvantages

  • Developers may not be able to use a full range of tools e.g. relational databases
  • Developers may sometimes find themselves locked to a particular platform, however most PaaSes are generally lock-in free

Cloud9 IDE is an online integrated development environment, it supports hundreds of programming languages, including C, C++, PHP and Ruby.

Enables developers to start coding immediately with pre-configured workspaces however a valid credit card is required upon sign up.

Founded in 2010, and based in San Francisco and Amsterdam, Cloud9 IDE was a privately held company, it was acquired by Amazon in July 2016.
