Communication as a Service (CaaS) - Gmail

Communications as a Service (CaaS) is an outsourced enterprise communications solution that can be leased from a single vendor. Such communications can include voice over IP , instant messaging, collaboration and videoconference applications using fixed and mobile devices.

Why CaaS?

  • The collaborative tools that cloud-based communications services provide make it much easier to get everyone on the same page
  • Facilitates business growth
